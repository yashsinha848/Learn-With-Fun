Foods to avoid

Cold Drinks

Avoid giving cold drinks to your young child since it may result in asthma or bronchitis.

Ice Cream

Avoid giving Ice Cream to your young child since it may result in asthma or bronchitis in later part of life.

Suggested Food

Leafy Green Vegetable

Spinach , Cabbage

Foods containing Vitamin C

Citrus Fruits- Orange, lemons, limes, grapefruit.
Potatoes, Brocolli , Brussel Sprouts , Peppers,Strawberries .
